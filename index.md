# Privacy Policy for Wordy Birdie

**Effective Date:** July 13, 2026

Wordy Birdie is developed and published by **The Oblong Studio**.

## Information We Collect

Wordy Birdie stores gameplay progress on your device to provide game functionality.

This may include:

- Current run progress
- Turns
- Seeds
- Eggs
- Bird collection
- Equipped birds
- Game settings
- Other gameplay progress

## Analytics

Wordy Birdie uses **Google Firebase Analytics** to improve gameplay and understand how the game is used.

Analytics may collect:

- Device information
- App version
- Gameplay events
- Session duration
- Performance information

## Advertising

Wordy Birdie includes optional rewarded advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**.

Watching advertisements is completely optional.

Google AdMob may collect information necessary to provide and measure advertising.

## In-App Purchases

Wordy Birdie offers optional in-app purchases using **Google Play Billing**.

Payments are securely processed by Google Play.

The Oblong Studio does not receive or store your payment card information.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses:

- Google Play Services
- Google Play Billing
- Google Firebase Analytics
- Google AdMob

## Data Sharing

The Oblong Studio does not sell your personal information.

## Children's Privacy

Wordy Birdie is intended for a general audience.
